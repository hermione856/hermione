About KDJ Salon

Founded in 2010 by three passionate beauty entrepreneurs—Kumari, Dinesh, and Jayanthi—KDJ Salon has grown from a small studio to Colombo's premier beauty destination. Our journey began with a shared vision to blend Sri Lanka's rich beauty traditions with international techniques and premium products.

Today, our award-winning salon serves thousands of clients annually, from local celebrities to international visitors seeking authentic Sri Lankan beauty experiences with a modern twist. Our commitment to excellence, continuous education, and personalized service has earned us recognition as Sri Lanka's Best Luxury Salon for three consecutive years.

  • Team of 20+ internationally certified professionals
  • Exclusive partnerships with premium global brands
  • Eco-friendly practices and cruelty-free products
  • Regular charity initiatives supporting women's education
